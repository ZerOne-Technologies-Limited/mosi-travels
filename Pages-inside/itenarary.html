ITINERARY 1 Luxury

Livingstone | The Royal Chundu

Welcome to Zambia! On your arrival at Livingstone Airport, you will connect with your private helicopter and fly over the Victoria Falls, into the gorge and along the river to The Royal Chundu where you will spend the first 2 nights of your tour.

Lower Zambezi National Park | Old Mondoro

This morning you will leave Livingstone and head to the Lower Zambezi. You will be transferred to Livingstone Airport for your flight to Lower Zambezi followed by transfers to Old Mondoro. You will spend the next 2 nights of your tour here.

Lower Zambezi National Park | Chiawa Camp

This morning you will be taken on a game drive transfer to your next camp in the Lower Zambezi, Chiawa Camp. You will spend the next 2 nights of your tour here.

South Luangwa National Park | Puku Ridge

Today you head to South Luangwa, dubbed by experts to be one of the greatest wild life sanctuary in the word. You will be transferred to the airstrip for your flight to flight to Mfuwe Airport. On arrival at Mfuwe Airport you will be met by a representative from Puku Ridge who will transfer you to the camp to stay for 3 nights.
