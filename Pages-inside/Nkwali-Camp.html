Description

Located on private land adjacent to and overlooking the South Luangwa National Park is one of the area’s premiere destinations, Nkwali Safari Camp. The camp’s captivating facilities are set against a wide bend in the Luangwa River and conveniently shaded by abundant ebony trees. With easy access to the game-rich Mfuwe Sector of the park via bridge or pontoon, this camp will leave you breathless with sightings of elephants and leopards, baboons and banded mongoose, just to name a few. You will discover excellent views of the lagoon and the river every area of this beautifully designed camp, from the stone patio outside your room to the thatched roof dining area. And as Nkwali is a relatively intimate camp, the staff here is incredibly attentive; you are sure to be dazzled by their unparalleled attention to detail and personalized service.
